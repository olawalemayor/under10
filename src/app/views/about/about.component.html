ABOUT US

IN THE INTIAL STAGES

We started as a community project showcasing the Udemy API as it was a front-end project for Angular development.

WITH TIME...

The project bgan to have some meaning in it as it was a great source of information for people trying to acquire affordable skills after the COVID-19 pandemic.

The Basic Idea

There are almost 8 billion people in the world, it makes it almost impossible to excel at a lot of things. Platforms like Udemy, Pluralsight and Udacity have resources that can level that scale.

Among several problems, one problem that hinders access to these plaform resources is the cost. That is where we come in...

What We're Doing

We are providing you with information from top-level platforms, information about courses that can take you to the next level and build up your career at affordable prices

Are We Making Profit?

No, not at this time. We are not profit oriented, our mission is to lead people out of the economic shambles of the COVID-19 epidemic and build up themselves for a better future.
